Reviseabl

Reviseabl is a Ruby on Rails web application that allows students to respond to short writing prompts and receive instant feedback.

It was conceived as the spiritual successor to SAGrader and utilizes the same core grading technology.

It is still in the alpha stage of development.

Student and instructor UIs are kept simple.

On the class view students can see their current and past assignments along with relevant data.

An instructor's main options are to view their gradebook, roster and list of all assignments.

They can also view basic performance statistics about their class. These include metrics like average scores both overall and by question, the percentage of improvement from revisions and specific students that rank high and low in various categories.

These statistics are computed daily in a Rake task.

When a student begins work on an assignment they are presented with a single question at a time. They write their response and receive feedback within a few seconds.

Students view a grading overlay while waiting for their feedback. Reviseabl submits the student's text to a REST API based on the core technology of SAGrader.

Once a student receives full credit (or gives up after three tries) they move on to the next question in the assignment.

Reviseabl also provides a UI to view the course's related textbook.

Textbook creators provide the the e-book in LaTeX and a backend script creates the HTML version.

The custom e-book viewer supports simple navigation between chapters and sections.

Reviseabl uses Stripe's checkout.js to accept payment for access.

Reviseabl supports the Learning Tools Interoperability (LTI) standard.

Instructors log in to their LMS and can create an assignment that links to a Reviseabl assignment.

When students log in to their LMS they can submit to Reviseabl assignments without leaving the LMS. Their assignment grades are automatically sent back to the LMS.

Reviseabl is responsive. All instructor and student pages can be used from any device.
